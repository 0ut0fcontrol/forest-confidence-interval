forestci.due¶

duecredit is a framework conceived to address the problem of inadequate citation of scientific software and methods. It automates the insertion of citations into code. We use it here to refer to the original publication introducing the method we have implemented.

See https://github.com/duecredit/duecredit/blob/master/README.md for examples.
